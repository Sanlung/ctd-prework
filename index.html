How to get Star Wars character and film information

Begin by clicking the down arrow in the selection box below to seclect a character whose info you want to see. The selected character's Star Wars profile will be displayed, along with links to all the episodes which feature the chcaracter. You can click on the link to any episode to see information about the film and, while in episode view, click the link to any character featured by the episode. You can also continue to select character names from the selection box to see their profiles.
